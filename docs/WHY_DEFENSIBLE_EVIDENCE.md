# Why this portfolio prioritizes defensible evidence (not hype)

Self-driving labs and autonomous discovery claims have drawn scrutiny when reproducibility and novelty claims are contested. Recent reporting highlights corrections and ongoing debates around high-profile autonomous lab results.

The portfolio’s positioning is therefore intentionally conservative:
- We do not claim “accelerate discovery.”
- We claim: **make end-to-end systems auditable, replayable, and defensible**.
